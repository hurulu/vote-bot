OUR CLUB:

Ingle Farm Junior Soccer Club

SPREAD THE WORD:

Share this URL: www.cenovis.com.au/win-the-goods-entry/vote/115

WHAT WOULD WE BUY?

Inflatable Soccer Pitch Hand cart for taking nets/ balls/ equipment to the pitches on game day. (This would make the coaches job a whole lot easier!)

WHY SHOULD WE WIN?

Our club has been a strong community member since 1971. We have nurtured many young community members many of whom have gone on to represent the state and our Country as valued soccer stars. (eg John Aloisi, Racheal Quigley & brothers Dylan & Ryan McGowan). We have members who have been with us for up to 30 years as players and now volunteer coaches & committee members. We have started gaining a growing number of new members from very diverse backgrounds. Many are new to our country and are welcomed by the players and volunteers as part of the community and followers of this great international sport. The growing number of new citizens to our shores is seeing our numbers starting to rapidly grow. We recently attended an expo where we had a great deal of interest from many parents for their children to join our club as we have a friendly and family orientated reputation. Not just about winning but about having fun and being a part of the local community. We will be attending more local events at schools and locally run events in the near future and the presence of a bright inflatable soccer pitch would be a great way to introduce the young players and have some fun. The Clubs motto ' We do it for the kids" was coined by one of the Club's favourite son's, the late Geoff Cardozo. We are very proud of this. Thank you & here's to the future of the world game in South Australia!
